How A Routine Can Help

An exercise routine with bodyweight and minimal equipment can be very effective for improving muscle strength and the body's mobility. Jump rope and yoga promote cardiovascular health, and tone muscles across the entire body. Adding weighted exercises to an exercise routine can increase muscle strength while improving stability. Resistance bands and tools such as ab rollers can also be just as beneficial as lifting weights.
